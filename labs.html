Labs

Hands-on labs and exercises focused on blue-team analysis, network traffic inspection, and digital forensics. These labs emphasize skill development and investigative thinking rather than full project ownership.

Blue Team / SOC Labs

  • TryHackMe — SOC and Blue Team–focused rooms (log analysis, detection, alert triage)
  • Windows event log analysis to identify abnormal user behavior and suspicious activity
  • IOC identification from structured log datasets

Network & Traffic Analysis Labs

  • Wireshark PCAP analysis to identify open ports, services, and suspicious traffic patterns
  • Protocol analysis (TCP, UDP, DNS, HTTP/HTTPS)
  • Conversation and statistics views to establish traffic baselines

Digital Forensics Labs

  • File system analysis (NTFS, exFAT)
  • $MFT record parsing and metadata interpretation
  • Artifact examination using FTK Imager and hex editors
  • Timeline reconstruction and evidence interpretation

CTF & Competitive Labs

  • National Cyber League (NCL) challenges
  • MetaCTF challenges
  • Timed problem-solving across networking, forensics, and security analysis
